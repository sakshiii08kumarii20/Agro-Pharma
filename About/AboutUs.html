Who we are ?

We are an Agro Homeopathic Pharmaceutical Company with a vision for creating a 100% organic and non toxic agricultural eco system.
Manakkal Agro Pharma is an agricultural homoeopathic pharmaceutical company situated in Kakkadampoyil, Calicut, Kerala, India. The company has its own 15 acres of agricultural land-named “Manakkal Orchard” wherein all of its Agro Homeopathic medicines are tested and achieved great success.
Manakkal Orchard
Manakkal Orchard is situated in the heart of Kakkadampoyil, a well known hilltop tourist destination of Kerala. It is full of various types of crops and trees fertilised with our own Agro homoeopathic medicines. Manakkal Agro pharma's products are well experimented and successfully proven here.
Our Founder
Dr. A.I. Hidayathullah, who has been actively working in the field of homeopathy for over three decades in Malappuram district, Kerala, is the founder of Manakkal Agro Pharma. Manakkal AgroPharma and all its products are the result of his 10-year-long relentless research in agricultural homeopathy.
