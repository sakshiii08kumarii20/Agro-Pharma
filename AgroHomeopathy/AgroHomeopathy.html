1. Definition of Agro Homeopathy

Agro homeopathy is the use of highly diluted homeopathic remedies to treat plant diseases, enhance soil quality, and promote healthy plant growth.

2. Principles of Agro Homeopathy

  • Law of Similars: "Like Cures Like" principle.
  • Minimum Dose Concept: Remedies are highly diluted.
  • Individualization: Specific remedies for different plant conditions.
  • Vital Force: Plants have an energy force that can be stimulated.

3. Applications of Agro Homeopathy

  • Pest and Disease Control
  • Soil Regeneration
  • Growth Stimulation and Yield Improvement
  • Weather Resilience
  • Germination and Seed Treatment
