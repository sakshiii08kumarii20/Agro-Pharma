Better Growth And Better Yield

Bridging nature and science for healthier lives

We are an Agro Homeopathic Pharmaceutical Company with a vision for creating a 100% organic and non-toxic agricultural ecosystem.

Manakkal Agro Pharma is an agricultural homoeopathic pharmaceutical company situated in Kakkadampoyil, Calicut, Kerala, India. The company has its own 15 acres of agricultural land named “Manakkal Orchard” wherein all of its Agro Homeopathic medicines are tested and achieved great success.

Our top selling product

Boost your crops' nutrient absorption naturally with proven homeopathic remedies-safe, chemical-free, cost-effective, and eco-friendly. Grow stronger, healthier plants for truly nutritious food.

AgroShield

100ml: ₹250

AgroShield is a broad-spectrum fungicide that controls a wide range of fungal diseases in crops. It is a systemic fungicide that is absorbed by the plant and translocated to the site of infection, providing long-lasting protection.

CropMax

500g: ₹600

CropMax is a plant growth regulator that promotes healthy plant growth and development. It increases crop yields, improves fruit quality, and enhances plant resistance to disease.

WeedOut

500ml: ₹800

WeedOut is a selective herbicide that controls a wide range of broadleaf weeds and grasses. It is a systemic herbicide that is absorbed by the plant and translocated to the site of action.
